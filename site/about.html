    Reading should be for everyone

    OrganicReader exists to make written content accessible, natural, and enjoyable for everyone through the power of on-device AI.

    Why OrganicReader exists

    Most text-to-speech apps feel robotic, require internet, charge subscriptions, or compromise your privacy by sending documents to the cloud. OrganicReader was built to challenge all of that.

    We believe that listening to a document should feel as natural as having someone read to you. That your private documents should stay private. And that powerful tools should be accessible to everyone, not just those who can afford a monthly fee.

    OrganicReader processes everything on your device using neural TTS models. No cloud calls, no subscriptions, no data collection. Just you and your documents.

    Our Values

    What we stand for

    The principles that guide every decision we make in building OrganicReader.

    Open Source

    OrganicReader is fully open source under GPL-3.0. Every line of code is transparent, auditable, and open for community contributions. We believe in building in public.

    Privacy-First

    Your documents are personal. OrganicReader processes everything on-device with zero cloud calls. We don't collect data, track usage, or see your content. Ever.

    User-First Design

    Every feature is designed around what readers actually need. Clean interface, intuitive controls, and thoughtful defaults. No dark patterns, no upsells, no clutter.
