    Everything you need to listen

    Powerful on-device AI meets thoughtful design. Every feature is built to make reading through listening feel natural and effortless.

    Voice Engine

    Natural voices that don't sound like robots

    OrganicReader uses Piper ONNX neural text-to-speech models to generate speech that sounds remarkably human. Choose from over 30 voices across a dozen languages, each trained on real human speech patterns.

    • 30+ neural voices across 12+ languages
    • All processing happens on your device
    • Adjustable speed without quality loss
    • Real-time sentence-level highlighting
    Format Support

    Import anything, listen everywhere

    Whether it's a research paper, novel, class notes, or web article, OrganicReader handles it all. Import from multiple sources and formats, and let the unified reader take care of the rest.

    • PDF, EPUB, DOCX, and TXT files
    • Web links and URLs
    • Photos and camera scans with OCR
    • Direct text input for quick listening
    Smart Library

    Your documents, organized

    Keep all your reading material in one place. Search, filter by format, and see your progress at a glance. The smart library makes it easy to find what you need and pick up where you left off.

    • Search across all your documents
    • Filter by format: PDF, DOCX, TXT, EPUB
    • Per-document progress tracking
    • Quick resume from continue reading
    Playback

    Full control over how you listen

    Adjust playback speed, skip forward or back, and bookmark important passages. The TTS pipeline pre-generates audio chunks for seamless playback, and speed adjustments happen at the player layer for instant response.

    • Variable speed playback control
    • Skip forward and backward 10 seconds
    • Bookmark passages for later
    • Scrubber for precise navigation
    Motivation

    Build a reading habit that sticks

    OrganicReader tracks your daily reading activity and builds streaks to keep you motivated. See your weekly listening hours, track how many documents you've consumed, and watch your progress grow over time.

    • Daily reading streaks with visual tracker
    • Weekly listening hour summaries
    • Customizable daily reading goals
    • Optional notification reminders
    Design

    Beautiful, distraction-free interface

    Four carefully crafted themes, six font options with adjustable sizing, and a clean layout that puts your content front and center. OrganicReader is designed to be a joy to use, whether you're reading or listening.

    • Light, Dark, Sepia, and Organic themes
    • Six font families with adjustable sizing
    • Customizable accent colors
    • Clean, minimal layout focused on content
    Technology

    Built with cutting-edge tech

    OrganicReader leverages the latest in mobile development and on-device machine learning to deliver a fast, private experience.

    React Native

    TypeScript-powered cross-platform framework for native mobile performance

    Sherpa ONNX

    On-device neural TTS engine delivering human-like speech without cloud calls

    Track Player

    Robust audio playback with queue management and background audio support

    Privacy-First

    Zero cloud dependencies means your documents and data never leave your device
